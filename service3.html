    PunchOut

    The PunchOut solution of Chemicalin provides an integration layer that enables you to initiate a transaction within your ERP environment and make orders easily and quickly.

    1.Access real-time marketing data and information

    Integrating with the Chemicalin sourcing platform offers you instant access to the availability and best prices of over 400,000 products in the marketing. You can also be able to avoid pricey procurement decisions that cost more time and budget than needed by knowing the latest products available to purchase in the market.

    2.More effective cost control and expenses tracking

    With an integrated ordering system, order information is collected online so that order accuracy is ensured. This removes difficulty in managing and tracking scattered orders, reducing the possibility of errors.

    3.Improve the productivity automatically

    PunchOut solutions of Chemicalin allows you to streamline the whole procurement process through automated ordering confirmation, error messaging and status updates. Shipment acknowledgement is also immediately and automatically forwarded to you. You can finally focus on the work that matters most.
