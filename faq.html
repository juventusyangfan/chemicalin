    FAQ
    1.Inquiries & Quotations

    1）How soon can I receive quote, order confirmation, invoice etc., from Chemicalin?

    A: Chemicalin respond to inquiries received both online and offline. Quotes are usually offered within 12 business hours. Online inquiries are processed even faster. Quotes are available through emails as well as your Chemicalin account.
    Order confirmation are sent immediately after payment received. For companies enjoying Chemicalin’s credit term, order confirmation are issued instantly after credit limit verification, usually within hours.
    Proforma Invoices (PI) are available instantly after quotes. Commercial Invoices (CI) are available after order confirmation. Both can be provided by your Chemicalin primary contact upon request or be directly downloaded from your own Chemicalin account.

    2）How can I know the expected dispatch date/lead time/delivery date?

    A: Chemicalin provides expected dispatch date at the time of your visit to product pages, as well as on each quotation. For example, when you visit CAS 6221-04-1 on May 3th, 2019 and see “shippable on 5-5-2019”, it means this product is ready to ship out in two days. The actual dispatch date will be decided upon your order confirmation date."
    Lead time can be calculated by comparing the “shippable date” to your actual visit date to Chemicalin.com or the date you received the specific quotation from us.
    Delivery date will be greatly decided upon ship-to country and the efficiency of customs and local delivery services. Chemicalin can only provide estimated delivery date from history orders.

    3）Can I have a consolidated shipment or partial shipments?

    A: Based on the number of items ordered and expected dispatch date as well as on whether the compounds are hazardous, consolidated shipments and partial shipments can be provided upon request. Please clarify your request at any stage before earliest dispatch date. Your primary contact at Chemicalin shall help you adjust shipping costs and plan dispatch schedules.
    For checking outs directly online from shopping-cart, consolidation of shipments will be automatically performed and show you a consolidated price in the shopping-cart when you check out.

    4）How to find the chemical I'm looking for?

    A: Chemicalin supports online search of our catalog products. If you know the CAS number or chemical name of the product you are looking for, you can use either to start a search, although using CAS number will always be more precise.
    If you do not have this information or can't find a match in our catalog, please post your inquiry after clicking the "Inquiry" Button on the navigation bar. In fact, you can post multiple inquiries easily with one submission.
    If your desired product doesn't have a CAS number, please feel free to reach us at support@ichemical.com and ask for an email quote. We will reach out to you immediately after receiving your information.
    Last but not least, make sure to sign up so we can process your request more efficiently!
    2.Pricing & Credit Terms

    1）What if I have preferred incoterm?

    A: Chemicalin caters to a variety of incoterm requirements.
    FOB: You can add product items to shopping cart and fill in your courier account number. Shipping fees will be automatically waived.
    CIF/DAP: You need to choose ship-to country first on top right corner of any webpage. The right pricing will be automatically applied. For inquiries, please put in the note of your desired incoterm.
    For other incoterms (CIP/DDP/CFR/FCA/EXW), please mention it in the "note" of your inquiries and we shall cater to your specific incoterms need when we offer you quotations.

    2）How can my company/organization earn Chemicalin credit term?

    A: Chemicalin requires advance payment for first orders from new accounts. Credit terms are granted upon user application. Please ask your Chemicalin Primary Contact for the Credit Application Form (CAF).
    Accounts with prior history of overdue payments will lose credit terms. For more questions, please write to your primary contact at Chemicalin.
    3.Order & Payment

    1）How to order from Chemicalin?

    A: To confirm order after receiving email quotations, simply log into your Chemicalin account, go to "My Inquiry" and proceed to order with your quoted items. If your organization requires other documents before an order can be placed, please notify your primary contact at Chemicalin to seek further assistance.
    If the product you are interested in is already online with pricing info of specific packages, you can make a purchase by adding to cart and checking out online.
    If your organization enjoys Chemicalin credit terms, check-out is even easier through shopping cart. Just choose wire transfer with credit terms and you are set to go.

    2）How can I ask for a quote for custom packages?

    A: After logging in, you can request for quotes in one of the two ways:
    1. Search product and click “Get a quote”;
    2. Click on the “Inquiry” button on the navigation bar of any webpage and follow instructions on the inquiry page. Quotes will be sent to your registered email address once available. You can also review them in your own Chemicalin “My Account” section.

    3）How to make a payment to Chemicalin?

    A: Payment are accepted only through offline means at this moment, although online credit card payment will be enabled in a few months. For now, to confirm order from quotations or to check out from shopping-cart and if your organization doesn’t enjoy Chemicalin credit term, please ask your primary contact at Chemicalin to provide banking details for wire transfer.
    4.Product Information

    1）Where to access COA, MSDS and other product related documents?

    A: Please mention in your inquiry request of COA, MSDS or other documents. You will be provided these documents together with quotation and/or before order dispatch in emails or hard copy with actual shipment.
    If there are certain index you require, please let us know as well to include in the technical documents.

    2）What is the usual shelf life of Chemicalin products?

    A: Shelf life for each product is substantially different. Please ask your primary contact to inquire on shelf life for specific products.
    5.After Purchase

    1）How to track my order?

    A: After order confirmation, you can see the status of order in your account on Chemicalin.com. A dispatch notice email will be sent to your registered email address.
    If you are checking on customization projects, there is a progress update module for each of your orders. You'll find access to synthetic routes, the interim reports, and tests on intermediates and final products.
    Tracking numbers are also available in your order list. You can log in and track your orders any time if the dispatch email is somehow lost.
    Your primary contact and account representative will be able to assist in customs clearance for international orders. So do let them know if you encountered any trouble.

    2）How to cancel an order?

    A: At Chemicalin, we take great pride in our product quality and service. Therefore, each order cancellation request will be handled one-on-one with our account managers. Please do give them a chance to help you make amends. The more details you provide, the easier it'll be for them to identify the problem and offer technical suggestions.

    3）How to return a product back to Chemicalin?

    A: Chemicalin’s Shanghai Warehouse is the only location to receive returned goods. To avoid confusion, please ask your primary contact for “Return Material Authorization (RMA)” and fill the form with necessary information. Shipping address will be provided to you after RMA approved by Chemicalin. Refund (if apply) will be processed immediately after returned goods received by Chemical’s Warehouse.
    Chemicalin will bare shipping charges and handling fees for returning goods only when product quality is proven compromised within three working days of the delivery date..
    6.Contacting Chemicalin

    1）How can my company become an Chemicalin supplier?

    A: Please write to support@Chemicalin.com with an introduction of your company. One of our supply chain management team will be in touch shortly.

    2）How can I reach an Chemical account representative?

    A: If this is your first attempt to contact Chemicalin or if you are not happy with your current primary contact, please write to support@Chemicalin.com for any trouble you might have. Our response rate is 100% within the first 12 working hours.
